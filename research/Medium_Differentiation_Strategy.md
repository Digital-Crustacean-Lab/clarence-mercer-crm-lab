# Medium Article Strategy: The Mercer Differentiation Series

## Article 1: From Sentiment to Intent (The CLV Revolution)
- **Baseline Comparison**: Most studies focus on star ratings or sentiment (Positive/Negative).
- **The Mercer Edge**: 
    - Introduction of **Intent Classification**. Why a "frustrated" review is a higher-value lead than a "generic positive" one.
    - Integration of **Market Basket Analysis** into CLV. Predicting *what* they will buy next, not just *when*.
- **Added Value**: A predictive matrix showing high-intent churn risks vs. low-intent loyalists.

## Article 2: Defeating the Digital Ghost (Forensic CRM Defense)
- **Baseline Comparison**: Standard fraud detection uses supervised black-box models (XGBoost).
- **The Mercer Edge**: 
    - **Physical Reality Audit**: Implementing the "Impossible Travel" geographical velocity check.
    - **Numerical Fingerprinting**: Solving the specs-clash problem (iPhone 15 vs 15 Pro) that pure NLP misses.
- **Added Value**: A blueprint for a "Self-Defending CRM" that uses physical world constraints to verify digital transactions.

## Article 3: The Game Theory of Marketing (Fair-Share Attribution)
- **Baseline Comparison**: Most research stops at funnel visualization or Last-Click statistics.
- **The Mercer Edge**: 
    - **Shapley Value Implementation**: Treating marketing channels as cooperative players in a Nobel-prize winning game theory framework.
    - **Time-Decay Weighting**: Adjusting Shapley values based on the recency of the touchpoint.
- **Added Value**: An "Efficiency Frontier" chart showing which channels are truly undervalued (The Hidden Gems).
